OpenNode OS

ISOs, appliances etc.

  • Sourceforge repository: http://sourceforge.net/projects/opennode
  • Turnkey Linux appliances for OpenNode: http://sourceforge.net/projects/turnkeylinux/files/opennode
  • CentOS6-to-OpenNode conversion script: http://opennodecloud.com/download/centos6-to-opennode6.sh

RPM repositories for CentOS 6

  • Stable: http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/opennode
  • Development: http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/opennode-dev
  • Patched OpenStack Havana: http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/openstack-havana

These repositories can be added to your system by running these commands: 

rpm -Uvh http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/opennode-release.rpm

curl http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/openstack.repo > /etc/yum.repos.d/opennode-openstack.repo
rpm --import http://opennodecloud.com/centos/6/RPM-GPG-KEY-OpenNode
