jostmart commented January 16, 2013

This struk me yesterday on several servers. What I found out was that since I have multipath, that created problems. lsof showed multipath using my SSD-device. So I added that device to blacklist in multipath.conf, then everything started to work as expected. None of the tools in flashcache worked as intended during the problems (flashcache_load, destroy, dmsetup status not showing the device, flashcache_create refusing to create).

And as the previous speaker said, check that your SSD haven't changed name. udev-rules would fix that.
